12-week progression
Phase 1 · Weeks 1–4 · Currently here
FOUNDATION — Build the habit
4 workout days/week. Peloton Zone 2–3 endurance. Ab roller from knees only. All bungee movements slow and controlled. Goal: your body adapting to regular training. Expected weight loss: 1–2 kg. Expected energy gain: significant by week 3.
Phase 2 · Weeks 5–8
BUILD — Increase intensity
5 workout days/week. Add HIIT intervals on Peloton. Ab roller from standing attempts begin. Bungee volume increases — add 1 extra set per exercise. Goal: visible muscle definition starting. Expected weight loss: 2–3 more kg. Saturday workout extends to 45 min.
Phase 3 · Weeks 9–12
SHRED — Hollywood finish
Peloton HIIT 3×/week. Bungee supersets (two exercises back-to-back, no rest). Ab roller from standing, full extension. Add 5 min morning walk before workout. Calorie deficit tightens to 600 kcal/day. Final 4 weeks push toward 78 kg target. Diet + training synchronized for peak physique.

Why this works
THE SCIENCE FOR A DESK WORKER
🚴
Peloton Zone 2 — the fat-burning engine
Zone 2 cardio (conversational pace) trains mitochondria to burn fat as fuel. 15 min/day, 3×/week produces measurable metabolic improvement within 4 weeks for sedentary individuals. More effective for fat loss than the same time in Zone 4–5.
⚡
Bungee resistance — the muscle builder
Resistance bands (bungee included) produce equivalent hypertrophy to free weights for untrained individuals — proven by 2019 meta-analysis. The variable resistance curve actually makes bungee superior for shoulder exercises. Perfect for your equipment situation.
🔁
Ab roller — the core compressor
EMG studies show ab roller produces highest rectus abdominis activation of any core exercise — higher than crunches, planks, or leg raises. 8 weeks of rollouts produces visible abdominal definition even without weight loss.
💪
3 lb dumbbells — the Hollywood trick
Celebrities use light weights at high reps (20–30) for shoulder and arm definition because it builds muscle density without bulk. At your current fitness level, 3 lbs at 20 reps creates genuine hypertrophy stimulus. This is a feature, not a limitation.
🪑
NEAT — the hidden calorie burner
Non-Exercise Activity Thermogenesis (fidgeting, desk breaks, walking) accounts for 200–400 kcal/day in active people versus sedentary. Your 4 desk breaks + lunch walk adds this without any dedicated workout time. Over 12 weeks: ~15,000 kcal extra burned.
